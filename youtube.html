Introduction

Youtube is a social media platform which is made for anyone to upload their videos publicly, as well as share, comment, like and view content without restrictions. Youtube can be accessed as an application through your phone as well as on PCs. Any individual can search for and watch videos themselves and follow / subscribe to channels they enjoy. Youtube is completely free! It was created by Jawed Karim, Steve Chen, and Chad Hurley. Former PayPal employees.

Did you know?

• According to the founders, the idea of Youtube began in a dinner party in 2004; born from their frustration of lack of video sharing online at the time.

• It started out as a garage project, in a garage!

•The first ever video on youtube was created on April 2005 titled "Me at the zoo" starring one of the creators Jawed Karim.
