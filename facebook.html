Introduction

Facebook can be used on your phone, Laptop or pretty much any device with a connection to the internet. It is used as a form of entertainment for many, as people post videos and share stuff about their daily lives. Facebook offers more than just entertainment value though. It can help you meet people, form relationships and even promote a business.
