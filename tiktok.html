Introduction

Tik-tok is a social media platform for creating, sharing, discovering short music videos. The app musical.ly was used by young people as an outlet to express themselves through dancing, singing and comedy. The TikTok app allows users to create 15 second videos, soundtracks by music clips. They can also connect clips together to create videos up to 60 seconds long. TikTok is available in over 150 countries and has over 1 billion viewers. Tiktok has been downloaded over 123 million times in the United States alone.
