Registering on the app

1

You don’t have to register for TikTok to use the app, although it is recommended that you do so that you can fully enjoy the app as the videos you see will be tailored to your preferences. To register for TikTok, you must download the app from the App Store (for iPhone/iPad) and the Play Store (for Androids). TikTok is also available as a website but you cannot create an account this way.

2

Locate the app on the app store and download it so it shows up on your home screen.

3

You can register with any previous account such as your gmail, facebook etc.. but if you want to keep it private select "use phone or email".

4

If you want to register with another account you have to choose your date of birth and afterwards your phone numer or email. After you've done that will be sent a verification email or text from your phone. Congratulations you are now a member of Tiktok!

How to use the Application

This is the main nav bar which sits on the bottom of the screen. From here you can access your profile and also use the discover tool to search certain video topics. The discover tool has many perks such as trending videos / hashtags.

From here you can browse through many different types of videos from all around the world. You can search things up such as "art" and click on the "users" from here it brings up top results/ users with education in their name/ videos/ sounds and hashtags.

From here I clicked onto videos and up popped thousands of art tutorials for me to browse through. Once you find a video you like you click into it to watch. From here you can access the video creators page by swiping right and browse through other videos they have. You can also use the tools to the right of the video (shown on right image) to like, comment and share to other people/platforms.

This is one of the creators video. You can see on the right side there are over 100,000 likes, there are so many people on this platform that it isn't hard to come across a video with over 3 million likes. TikTok is really simple once you get the hang of it. You can follow the artist by simply tapping the + icon below their profile picture on the right hand side.

To create your own Tic Tok you need to tap on the center tab to enter camera mode. From here you will be brought to a creative space to make a video. You can choose between making it 15seconds or 60seconds long. On the right side of the screen there is 6 icons.

After recording the video click next. From here you can add music and different effects to the video. It's a lot to take in but you can get extremely creative with your videos and it's worth it!
