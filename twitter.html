Introduction

Twitter was made on the 21st of march, 2006 the founders were Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Evan Williams and Biz Stone. Twitter is a social networking service were people from around the entire world can interact with each other and interact with messages which are also known as “tweets”. People that have made twitter accounts can post tweets and like other tweets and one of the unique features twitter has is a retweet where you can post someone elses tweet on your page so more people can see it, people that have not made an account can only read the tweets.

Did you know?

•There are over 300 million people that are signed up for twitter and still have active accounts!
•Around 350,000 tweets are pouring out every single minute on the social platform.

•The twitter logo that looks like a bird actually has a name, which is Larry.

•Barack Obama has the most twitter followers at 115 million followed by Justin Bieber with 111 million followers.
