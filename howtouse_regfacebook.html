Registering on the app

1

The facebook application is available on both android and apple systems, start by searching the app in each systems respective app store.For users wanting to sign up on a laptop , go to facebook.com and select “create new account and follow the instructions below.

2

You will be asked for basic information such as your name, date of birth , gender. You will finish setting up your account by supplying your mobile number or email address, creating a password and finally agreeing to facebooks terms of service

How to use the Application

You can do so many different things on facebook, you can look through your timeline which will be filled with posts created or shared by your friends. You can also take a look at your profiler and edit it as much as you want, you can share posts from within your timeline and they will automatically go to your homepage!

By making a status, you can change the background of it, include different gifs, stickers, etc.. You can also say where in the world you are and what you're doing! You can tag your friends or share your status to the public or you can make it so only one person can see it by messing around with the settings. There are so many different options that you can get lost in it.
