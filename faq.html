Frequently asked Questions

How do i get more followers?

First of all try to maintain your social media by following your friends and family members or people that you have been acquainted with before. Most of the people that follow me on my social media such as Instagram are people that I have been familiarized with. I don’t tend to follow people back unless I know them but it all depends on the person. Tagging your posts / photos helps a lot such as #f4f which means follow for follow which is a commonly used Instagram tag that helps with boosting up your followers. There are many ways to earn followers but my recommendation is just be yourself! You don’t have to have thousands of followers, being yourself will get you the platform you need, Share your opinions etc..

Do I have to be on every social media?

It all depends on yourself really, if you like the idea of social media you can easily set up an account and keep up with the trends. Creating an account is really helpful if you want to learn more about the platform and what it’s like. The majority of social media platforms you have to create an account in order to access certain things. Facebook has it so you have to register in order to look through content on the platform, so does Instagram!

Should I talk to people I don’t really know?

This is a really hard question to answer because you never know what kind of person is behind the screen, have you seen catfish the TV show? It almost never goes right in this situation. Connecting with random people gives us a pleasant feeling because we don’t know the person and it’s easier to connect online than talking to a stranger on the street. I feel like you should do some research before talking to someone because they might be faking who they are, and what they look like. I feel like you should keep your circle small when connecting with people, you should talk to people you know personally and people you’ve met before who you haven’t really talked to!
