About Us!

Honestly it’s just one person.. Hi! I’m Anna and I’m the person who created this website! I specifically designed this so that we would all get familiarized with social media tools that are mostly well known in the internet world. Throughout half of my life I’ve been addicted to Youtube because I liked how each individual person had their own content. I mostly watched video game tutorials and now I love the idea of book, film and music reviews. I don’t really go on twitter that much but when I do I mostly check out what’s trending that is to do with worldwide news. If you want like-minded people I suggest you check out reddit and what it’s about, I got a lot of information from that social media platform whenever my laptop bugged out on me or If I had any problems to do with electronics. I feel like a lot of people have facebook but sometimes it can get a bit too much, too many people share false information which is disturbing so I only tend to message my close friends and family whenever needs be. As for TIk Tok I am addicted and you’ll find that out once you get to make your own account. Anyway enough information about what social media I use, get cracking on that research and contact me if you need help!
