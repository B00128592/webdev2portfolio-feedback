Registering on the app

1

Once you type up twitter on google it will load you up to this page where you can either make an account or sign into an existing account, if the person already had an account they would head straight into twitter and wouldn’t see this page.

2

When you press sign up you will be introduced to this page and you have created a username for your account and you also have to either put your email address down or your phone number. The reason you have to do either one of them is because it will help twitter contact you just in case something happened to your account.

How to use the Application

On twitter as I mentioned the posts are called tweets. To tweet you would need to follow these next steps. Now write your post, it can be anything!

add any relevant hashtags that you may want to include and click tweet. Your tweet will afterwards appear on your homepage. If you click on the blue hashtags, they bring you to a page that shows all other tweets that include the same relevant word or phrase that you used.
